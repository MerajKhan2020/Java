Martial Arts Manager, is an application designed to assist managing a small martial arts school, or other similar business or club. The goal of such schools or clubs is usually not financially driven but often a self financing hobby. It is a way to give back to the wider community while fostering a close-knit community of friendships and training partners. The predominant business problem is to manage the minimum tasks needed to efficiently organise a small martial arts school in an easy to use manner while maintaining data ownership. This is where MAM assists the management of school to organise their day to day tasks, curriculum and activities and keeping records of them.
